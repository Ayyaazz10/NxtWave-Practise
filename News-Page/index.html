iB Cricket announces world's first
Virtual Reality Cricket League

iB Cricket and Viu together have announced World's first virtual reality Cricket League featuring 12 international cricketers, who will be competing aganist each other for the iB Cricket Super Over League title in Mumbai. Iconic cricketers like Virender Sehwag, Suresh Raina, Brendon McCullum will be competing in the league.
